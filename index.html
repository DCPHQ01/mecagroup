    • WHO ARE WE

    THE MECA GROUP stands as a dynamic conglomerate, seamlessly integrated into the sectors – AGROTECH, ENERGY and E-COMMERCE – to drive innovation, ecological stewardship, and technological advancement. Grounded in an unwavering dedication to sustainable and economic development, the MECA GROUP envisions a future wherein the integration of cutting-edge engineering, clean energy solutions, and digital commerce harmoniously converge to mold a flourishing and environmentally conscious society.

    Mission

    To become the leading provider of sustainable solutions anchored in unwavering environmental responsibility, ethical practices and an unrelenting pursuit of excellence, forging a thriving and prosperous Nation, a testament to Africa's limitless possibilities.

    Vision

    We envision a harmonious future where the convergence of cutting-edge engineering, clean energy, and E-commerce serves to elevate sustainable economic development and propels the creation of visionary, innovation- driven society.

    Dr. Iliyasu B. Gashinbaki

    Founder/GCEO

    Dr. Iliyasu B. Gashinbaki is an accomplished executive and visionary leader with vast experience in governance, strategic direction, and institutional transformation across both public and private sectors. He is the Founder and Group Chairman of MECA Group, a dynamic conglomerate driving innovation in AgroTech, Energy/Telecommunications, and E-Commerce. With a track record of value creation, revenue growth, and policy development, Dr. Gashinbaki also serves as Vice President of the Abuja Chamber of Commerce and Industry (ACCI) and chairs its Finance, Banking & Insurance Trade Group. As the Founder and Pioneer President of the Chartered Institute of Forensics and Certified Fraud Investigators of Nigeria (CIFCFIN), established by law in 2022, he is championing the professionalization of forensics and fraud investigation in Nigeria. His leadership continues to shape economic policy, sectoral reform, and digital transformation across Nigeria’s key industries.
